I’m from Melbourne. Before arriving at UCL I was a Lecturer at Monash University (2013-17), and a Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of Chicago (2015). I did my graduate studies at Oxford (2009-13) and Monash (2007-08). I’ve contributed to various programs which bring philosophy to a wider public, including the Stuart Low Trust, the Victorian Association for Philosophy in Schools, The Leeds Film and Philosophy Society, Humanities 21, the Drawing Room, and the Elucidations podcast.
